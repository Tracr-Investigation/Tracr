# Notifications

Tracr sends real-time notifications to keep you informed of activity on your investigations.

---

## Notification types

| Type | Trigger |
|---|---|
| Invitation | You have been invited to join an investigation |
| Status change | The status of an investigation you are a member of has changed |
| Mention | You have been mentioned in a document |
| System | Platform message |

---

## Accessing notifications

- **Bell icon** in the sidebar: shows the number of unread notifications. Click to access the full Notifications page.
- **Dashboard**: the 5 most recent notifications are displayed in the "Recent notifications" section.

---

## Clicking a notification

Clicking a notification in the list automatically marks it as read and navigates to the relevant investigation if applicable.

---

## Pending invitations

Invitations to join an investigation appear in the Notifications page as a card with **Accept** and **Decline** buttons. They are also visible and actionable directly from the **Dashboard**.

---

## Mark as read

- Click a notification to mark it as read individually.
- A **Mark all as read** button lets you mark all notifications at once.

---

## Real-time notifications

Notifications are delivered in real time via Socket.IO. No page reload is needed to receive a new notification.
