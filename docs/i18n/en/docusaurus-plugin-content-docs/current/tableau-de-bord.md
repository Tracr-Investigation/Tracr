# Dashboard

The dashboard is the home page of Tracr. It provides a summary view of your activity and the state of the platform.

---

## Stat cards

Four cards display in real time:

- **Active investigations**: total number of non-closed investigations you have access to
- **Open tasks**: number of tasks assigned to you that are not yet done
- **Overdue tasks**: assigned tasks whose due date has passed
- **Due this week**: tasks whose due date falls within the next 7 days

---

## Pending invitations

If you have received invitations to join investigations that you have not yet accepted, a block appears at the top of the dashboard listing each invitation.

For each invitation, you can:
- See the investigation name and the inviting user's username
- Click **Accept** to join the investigation
- Click **Decline** to reject it

The block disappears automatically once all invitations have been handled.

---

## Recent investigations

The **Recent investigations** section shows the 6 most recently viewed or modified investigations. Each row shows:

- The investigation's numeric identifier
- The title
- A "Collab" badge if you are not the owner
- Associated categories (up to 2 shown)
- The last modification date
- The status

Click a row to open the investigation directly. The **View all** link takes you to the full list.

---

## My tasks

The **My tasks** section lists all tasks assigned to you, across all investigations, in chronological order.

Each task shows:
- A color dot representing the priority (grey, blue, orange, red)
- The task title (strikethrough if done)
- The name of the source investigation
- The status
- The due date (in red if overdue)

Clicking a task navigates to the corresponding investigation. The **Calendar** link opens the calendar view of your tasks.

---

## Breakdown by status and category

The breakdown section shows two horizontal bar charts:

- **By status**: number of investigations per status, with each status's color
- **By category**: number of investigations per category (up to 6 categories), with each category's icon and color

These charts cover all your investigations, including those where you are a collaborator.

---

## Recent notifications

The **Recent notifications** section shows the 5 most recent notifications received. Unread notifications appear with a color dot and bolder text.

Clicking a notification marks it as read and navigates to the relevant investigation if applicable. The **View all** link opens the full Notifications page.
